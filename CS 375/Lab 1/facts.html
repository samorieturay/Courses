Interesting Facts About Red Pandas

  • Red pandas are native to the Himalayas and southwestern China.
  • Despite their name, they are not closely related to giant pandas.
  • They have a false thumb, which helps them grasp bamboo while eating.
  • Red pandas are mostly solitary and are active at dusk and dawn.
  • Their reddish-brown fur provides excellent camouflage among red moss and white lichens.
